# bikesharing
## Project Overview
We're analyzing New York Citi Bike using visualization tools to explore the viability of a bikesharing business in Des Moines, Iowa.

## Resources
- Data Source: [Citi Bike Data](https://www.citibikenyc.com/system-data), [201908-citibike-tripdata.csv.zip](https://s3.amazonaws.com/tripdata/201908-citibike-tripdata.csv.zip)
- Software: Python, Conda Jupyter Notebook, Tableau Public

## Summary
The data indicates that Citi Bike fares well in the month of August. The majority of the riders are younger, and generally the younger riders are most likely to rent bikes for longer. Depending on the demographic of Des Moines and tourists, we can further investigate by:
- determing what month is most popular amongst tourists in Des Moines,
- using weather data to determine what months of the year are most hospitable for a bikesharing business.
